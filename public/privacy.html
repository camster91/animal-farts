💨 Poot Party — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

Poot Party is designed for children. We take your family's privacy seriously. This policy explains what data Poot Party collects, what it does not collect, and how all data is stored entirely on your device.

1. What We Collect

Poot Party stores the following data locally on your device using IndexedDB (a browser-based local database). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it:

  • Kid profile name and emoji avatar — chosen by the parent or child when setting up a profile.
  • Voice recordings — recorded by the child when using the long-press feature to record a custom sound. Recordings are stored on-device only.
  • Heard count — a counter tracking how many sounds the child has played, stored per profile.
  • Error reports — if the app encounters an error, a anonymous report (containing no personal information, no recordings, no profile names) may be logged to our server for diagnostic purposes only.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

Poot Party does not collect, share, or monetize any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No advertising networks or third-party trackers
  • No cookies
  • No location data
  • No camera access (microphone access is used only for voice recordings, described in §3)
  • No social features (recordings cannot be shared to social media or with other users)
  • No data is sold or provided to any third party

3. Microphone Permission

Poot Party requests microphone access only when a child long-presses on an empty area of a scene to record a custom sound. This permission is never requested at any other time. Recorded sounds are stored locally in the browser's IndexedDB and are never uploaded to our servers automatically.

4. Where Data Lives

All kid data — profiles, recordings, and heard counts — is stored in IndexedDB inside the browser on the device where Poot Party is installed. This data is not synced to any server and is not accessible to us. Parents can delete all data for a profile at any time from the parent dashboard.

The only data that passes through our servers is anonymized error reports (see §1), which contain no personal information, recordings, or profile data.

5. Parental Controls

The parent dashboard (accessible at/parent, protected by a 4-digit PIN) allows parents to:

  • Delete any or all kid profiles and their associated recordings
  • Set daily recording limits
  • Set quiet hours to restrict when the app can be used
  • Control voice effect settings
  • Enable or disable Poot Party TV mode

All of these controls are local to the device and do not require an internet connection.

6. Children's Data (COPPA Compliance)

Poot Party is designed for children under13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All data stored by the app — profiles, recordings, and heard counts — is created and controlled by the parent or child on the local device. We have no way to access, review, or delete any data stored on a user's device.

If you are a parent and believe your child has created a profile using a personal name or any personal information, you can delete all profiles and data from the parent dashboard at /parent.

7. Your Rights

As a parent, you have the right to delete all data associated with any child profile at any time. This is done from the parent dashboard at/parent. Select the profile you wish to delete and confirm deletion — this permanently removes the profile, all recordings, and all heard-count data from the device.

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to request deletion of any data we may have collected through error reports, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through the app. We encourage parents to review this policy periodically.
