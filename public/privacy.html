💨 PootBox Privacy

No account needed

We don't ask for your name, email, or any account. Just open the app and start playing.

No data leaves your device

Your recordings and pages are stored only on this device using browser storage. We don't upload anything automatically.

Microphone

The microphone is only used while you are actively recording a sound. We never listen in the background. Recordings stay on your device and are never sent anywhere.

No ads, no analytics

We don't track you, we don't show ads, and we don't sell your data. PootBox is made for kids and families.
